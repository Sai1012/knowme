THOR:GOD OF THUNDER

Thor, the Norse god of thunder, as depicted in various mythologies and popular culture,
embodies strength, courage, and a sense of duty. In Marvel Comics
and the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU),
Thor is portrayed as a mighty warrior
and prince of Asgard, wielding the enchanted hammer Mjolnir.
Initially depicted as arrogant and impulsive,
Thor undergoes significant character development throughout his story arcs.
